OUR EVENTS

Join us on the various events organized by us

We organize various events from our store in the respective cities. The events may be some casual bike meet ups related to some particular bikes or may be some casual meetups for all our customers. The event may also be an launch event of any of our customized biks built by us. The events may also be bike roads trips organized by particular stores.
As we have organized a lot of events in the past since our stores have been opened we stick to our culture and go on arranging a lot of events every year. We have done a lot of events such as meetups, trips and much more which include road trip to Kashmir, Nepal, Gujarat, Kanyakumari, Pondicherry, Karwar, Goa and many more places. We also had meetups related to Harely Davidson motor company and Royal Enfield at Mumbai, Goa, Chennai and New Delhi. We also had coffee meetups for Triumph and KTM bikers at our Bangalore and Hyderabad stores.
We also had some launch events of our customized bikes at our Mysore and Hyderabad stores which developed and created a lot of enthusiasm among the whole nation, to which we received a very good response with a large crowd attending the event.
Here we have some quite fascinating and expert organizers with us who help us arrange and organize all the things that are needed for trips and meetups from food, stay, hotels, activities and all the necessary stuff.

Please kindly note on some of the points :

1. This website provides you only the details of the events.
2. Inorder to attend any meetup or join any events organized by us you need to visit the respective store which is organizing the event.
3. You need to provide all the necessary documents of the rider and the bike at the respective store which is organizing the event at the time of registration.
4. Following of all the guidelines and instructions given by the organizer is must.
5. All details are for security reasons and will be maintained by the company during the ride.
6. Bringing of bikes during the registring of an event is compulsory.
